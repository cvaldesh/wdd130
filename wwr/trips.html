Our Featured Adventures

Half-Day River Explorer

Perfect for families and first-timers, this trip offers a relaxing journey through stunning natural landscapes with gentle currents and breathtaking views. Enjoy a peaceful paddle and discover the local wildlife.

Full-Day Rapids Challenge

For those seeking a bit more excitement, our full-day adventure takes you through exhilarating Class III and IV rapids. Experience the thrill of whitewater with our experienced guides. Lunch is provided riverside.

Multi-Day Wilderness Trek

Immerse yourself in nature with our multi-day expedition. This advanced trip combines intense rapids with serene stretches, allowing for camping under the stars. All gear and meals are included for an unforgettable wilderness experience.
